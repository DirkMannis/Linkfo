# Linkfo User Guide

Welcome to Linkfo! This guide will help you get started with creating your AI agent persona and managing your links.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit [linkfo.me](https://linkfo.me) 
2. Click on "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Fill in your name, email, and password
4. Click "Create Account"

### Setting Up Your Profile

After creating your account, you'll be directed to your dashboard. Here you can:

1. Add a profile picture
2. Write a short bio
3. Start adding your links
4. Connect your social media accounts to create your AI agent persona

## Managing Your Links

### Adding Links

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click on the "Links" tab
3. Fill in the form with:
   - Link title (e.g., "My Website")
   - URL (e.g., "https://example.com") 
   - Icon (choose from available options)
   - Color (customize the link's accent color)
4. Click "Add Link"

### Editing Links

1. Find the link you want to edit in your links list
2. Click the edit icon
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Deleting Links

1. Find the link you want to delete in your links list
2. Click the delete icon
3. Confirm deletion

### Reordering Links

1. Go to your links list
2. Drag and drop links to change their order
3. The new order will be automatically saved

## Creating Your AI Agent Persona

### Connecting Content Sources

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click on the "Content Sources" tab
3. Click "Add Content Source"
4. Choose the source type (Twitter, Instagram, YouTube, or Blog/Website)
5. Enter your username or website URL
6. Click "Add Source"

### Updating Your Persona

After connecting your content sources:

1. Click "Update Persona from Sources"
2. The system will analyze your content to create your AI agent persona
3. This process may take a few minutes depending on the amount of content

### Viewing Your Persona

1. Go to the "Persona" tab
2. Here you can see:
   - Knowledge domains (topics you're knowledgeable about)
   - Communication style
   - Personality traits
   - Values and interests

### Testing Your AI Agent

1. Go to your public profile page
2. Click "Chat with my AI Agent"
3. Ask questions to see how your AI agent responds
4. The agent will use your persona to provide responses that match your style and knowledge

## Customizing Your Profile

### Changing Your Profile Information

1. Go to the "Settings" tab
2. Update your name, email, or bio
3. Click "Save Changes"

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to the "Settings" tab
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter your new password
4. Confirm your new password
5. Click "Update Password"

## Viewing Analytics

### Link Performance

1. Go to your dashboard
2. The top section shows your overall statistics
3. In the "Links" tab, each link shows its click count

### Visitor Engagement

1. Go to your dashboard
2. View the "Chat Interactions" statistic to see how many people have engaged with your AI agent

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does the AI agent learn about me?

The AI agent analyzes your social media posts, articles, and other online content to understand your knowledge domains, communication style, and personality traits.

### Can I customize what my AI agent says?

Currently, the AI agent automatically generates responses based on your online persona. In future updates, we plan to add more customization options.

### How often should I update my persona?

We recommend updating your persona whenever you add new content sources or after posting significant new content. This ensures your AI agent stays up-to-date with your latest interests and knowledge.

### Can visitors see my personal information?

No, visitors can only see the information you choose to share on your public profile. Your email address and other sensitive information are kept private.

### How do I share my Linkfo page?

Your public profile is available at `linkfo.me/yourusername`. You can share this link on your social media profiles, email signature, or anywhere else you want to direct people to your links.

### Can I use a custom domain?

Custom domains are available on our Pro plan. You can upgrade from your dashboard's "Settings" tab.

### How do I delete my account?

1. Go to the "Settings" tab
2. Scroll to the bottom to the "Danger Zone" section
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm deletion
